Hi! My name is Glenn van der Linden. I am
a Game Developer currently studying Open-IT
at Hogeschool Utrecht. I've loved games since
I was 3 years old and I always wanted to make my own,
but Unfortunately I didn't have the means to do so. I am
adept in the use of Unity and C#. I am currently learning
web development as well.
I finished a degree in Game Development, at the
Grafisch Lyceum in Utrecht.

I did internships at SyncVR and
the Ministry of Defense respectively!

The internship at Defense thought me alot
and I even got offered a job.
I made a quiz app for the new interns of Defense.
The rules where clear to me early on, so they
thought I would be the best person to create this app

For syncVR I mostly did Game Development on
small entertainment games for hospitalized people,
ofcourse in VR and mainly with the Pico G2 4K

I've been working with C# and Unity for 6 years now!
HTML and CSS are both very new for me, however
I've been having a lot of fun making and designing
my website.
I've been working on my self-reflection a lot, I find that I'm great with words on paper.
I am also pro-active and I try to deal with problem before they arise.

I've always believed that working is very important for personal growth.
